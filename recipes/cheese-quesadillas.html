Cheese Quesadillas

Description

Melted cheddar cheese in a folded up tortilla. Great on their own or dipped in salsa and sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • coarse kosher salt
  • vegatable oil
  1. Heat large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Toast both tortillas and set aside to assemble.
  3. Fill one half of each tortilla with cheese, leaving a half an inch on the edges.
  4. Fold both tortillas over the cheese.
  5. Brush the tops of both folded tortillas with oil and sprinkle with salt.
  6. Place both folded tortillas oiled side down in skillet.
  7. While first side is cooking oil and salt the top of the tortillas.
  8. After the bottom side is crispy and brown flip and cook the other side.
  9. Let rest five minutes then cut into slices and enjoy.
