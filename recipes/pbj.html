Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A classic American sandwich combining salty peanut butter and sweet Jelly on sandwich bread. This version calls for raspberry jelly, JIF peanut butter and white bread. Feel free to subsitute your favorite versions of the three ingredients.

Ingredients

  • white sandwich bread
  • raspberry jelly
  • JIF peanut butter
  1. Spread jelly on one piece of bread.
  2. Sread peanut butter on the other piece of bread.
  3. Combine the bread with the sides covered in the ingredients touching.
