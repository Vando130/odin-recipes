Bahn Mi

Description

This classic Vietnamese sandwich uses leftover rotisserie chicken for a quick, easy and delicous meal. Make ahead a quick pickle of radishes and carrots and a spicy sriracha mayo to make throwing together this sandwich a snap.

Taken from Andrea Nguyen's "The Bahn Mi Handbook" pg. 65

Ingredients

  • a small rotisserie chicken
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • canola oil
  • Hoagie rolls, hollowed out
  • pickled veggies
  • sriracha mayo
  • maggi sauce
  • cucumber, sliced
  • cilantro, chopped
  1. Pull apart meat from chicken into bite size pieces.
  2. Season chicken meat with salt and pepper.
  3. Reheat chicken over medium heat with a splash of canola oil.
  4. Fill bottom bread with chicken.
  5. Top with maggi sauce, pickled veggies, cucumber and cilantro.
  6. Smear top bread with sriracha mayo.
